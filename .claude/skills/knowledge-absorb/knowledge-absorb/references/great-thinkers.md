# Great Thinkers Per Domain — Knowledge Absorb Skill

Quick reference: Who are the giants in each field, what are they known for,
and who challenges them. Use when teaching from memory (no PDF provided).

---

## Psychology / Human Behavior

| Thinker | Known For | Best Work | Challenged By |
|---|---|---|---|
| Dale Carnegie | Human relations, likeability, influence | How to Win Friends and Influence People | Critics: too manipulative, surface-level charm |
| Robert Cialdini | 6 principles of influence | Influence; Pre-Suasion | Critiques of manipulative application |
| Jordan Peterson | Archetypes, responsibility, meaning, Jungian psychology | 12 Rules for Life; Maps of Meaning | Critics: political overgeneralization, gender claims |
| Viktor Frankl | Logotherapy — meaning-centered psychology | Man's Search for Meaning | Positive psychology movement (Seligman) |
| Carol Dweck | Growth vs. fixed mindset | Mindset | Replication concerns on some mindset studies |
| Daniel Kahneman | System 1 vs. System 2 thinking, cognitive bias | Thinking, Fast and Slow | Replication crisis: some studies haven't held |
| Carl Jung | Shadow, archetypes, collective unconscious | Man and His Symbols | Freudians, empirical psychologists |
| B.F. Skinner | Behaviorism, operant conditioning | Beyond Freedom and Dignity | Cognitivists (Chomsky's critique) |

## Philosophy / Stoicism / Meaning

| Thinker | Known For | Best Work | Challenged By |
|---|---|---|---|
| Marcus Aurelius | Stoic practice, duty | Meditations | Epicureans, existentialists |
| Epictetus | Dichotomy of control | Enchiridion, Discourses | Critics of passivity |
| Seneca | Time, urgency, Stoic living | Letters from a Stoic | Hypocrisy critiques (he was extremely wealthy) |

| Ryan Holiday | Stoicism applied to modern life | The Obstacle Is the Way; Ego Is the Enemy | Academic philosophers: "not real Stoicism" |
