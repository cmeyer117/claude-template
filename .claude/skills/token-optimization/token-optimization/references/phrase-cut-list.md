# Phrase Cut List

These phrases add zero signal. Cut them on sight.

## Opening Filler
- "Great question!"
- "That's a great point."
- "Certainly!"
- "Of course!"
- "Absolutely!"
- "Sure thing!"
- "Happy to help!"
- "Let me help you with that."
- "I'd be happy to..."
- "Great, let's dive in!"
- "Let's get started!"
- "I'll walk you through..."

## Transitional Padding
- "As I mentioned earlier..."
- "To recap what we discussed..."
- "As we established..."
- "Just to reiterate..."
- "In other words..." (unless genuinely clarifying)
- "Essentially what this means is..."

## Closing Filler
- "I hope this helps!"
- "Let me know if you have any questions!"
- "Feel free to ask if you need clarification."
- "Don't hesitate to reach out!"
- "Is there anything else I can help you with?"
- "Let me know if you'd like me to expand on any of these points."

## Hedge Padding (use sparingly, not reflexively)
- "It's worth noting that..."
- "It's important to keep in mind that..."
- "As you may already know..."
- "Of course, this depends on your specific situation..." (unless actually relevant)

## Meta-Commentary (usually cuttable)
- "I'm going to explain X by..."
- "Let me break this down for you."
- "Here's what I'll do:"
- "I'll now explain..."
- "To answer your question..."

## Apology Padding
- "I apologize for the confusion."
- "I'm sorry for any misunderstanding."
- "I apologize if that wasn't clear."
(One brief acknowledgment is fine. Don't dwell.)
