# Extended Authority Sources

For domains not covered in the main SKILL.md. Apply the same source hierarchy:
peer-reviewed → elite practitioners → institutional consensus → respected books.

---

## ⚖️ Law & Legal Research
- Cornell Law School (LII) — free legal resource, primary law
- Westlaw / LexisNexis — authoritative legal databases
- Supreme Court opinions — primary source for constitutional/federal law
- Nolo — plain-language legal guides (general public)
- Harvard Law Review — academic legal analysis

## 🏥 Health, Nutrition & Medicine
- PubMed / NIH — peer-reviewed medical research
- Mayo Clinic — clinical consensus, patient-facing accuracy
- Harvard Health Publishing — evidence-based health content
- New England Journal of Medicine — top-tier medical research
- Examine.com — supplement research database, no conflicts of interest

## 📈 Business & Entrepreneurship
- Harvard Business Review — management research and strategy
- MIT Sloan Management Review — innovation, leadership
- McKinsey Global Institute — industry research and trends
- Peter Drucker — management foundations
- Clayton Christensen — disruption theory (*The Innovator's Dilemma*)
- Jim Collins — organizational excellence (*Good to Great*, *Built to Last*)

## 🌍 Economics & Macro
- Federal Reserve Economic Data (FRED) — comprehensive economic datasets
- World Bank — global economic data
- IMF (International Monetary Fund) — global financial stability
- Milton Friedman — monetary economics
- John Maynard Keynes — macroeconomic theory
- Thomas Sowell — economics and social policy (*Basic Economics*)

## 🔬 General Science & Research
- Nature — top-tier peer-reviewed science
- Science — broad scientific research
- Google Scholar — academic paper search
- JSTOR — academic journal archive
- Cochrane Reviews — gold standard systematic reviews in medicine

## 🎯 Leadership & Self-Development
- Napoleon Hill — success principles (*Think and Grow Rich*)
- Stephen Covey — effectiveness frameworks (*7 Habits*)
- Robert Greene — strategy, power, mastery (*48 Laws*, *Mastery*, *33 Strategies*)
- Marcus Aurelius — stoic philosophy (*Meditations*)
- David Goggins — mental toughness, discipline
- Tony Robbins — peak performance, state management

## 🏦 Real Estate & Alternative Investing
- BiggerPockets — real estate investing community and research
- Robert Kiyosaki — cash flow investing (*Rich Dad Poor Dad*)
- Grant Cardone — commercial real estate, 10X framework
- National Association of Realtors — market data
- CoStar — commercial real estate data

## 📡 Emerging Tech & Web3
- a16z (Andreessen Horowitz) — tech venture research and essays
- CoinDesk / The Block — crypto/blockchain journalism
- Ethereum Foundation — blockchain research
- CB Insights — startup and tech market intelligence
